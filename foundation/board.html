Board and Auditors

In the constituent general assembly on August 16, 2002, the following officers were elected as board of the R Foundation:
Presidents:
Robert Gentleman, Ross Ihaka
Secretary General:
Friedrich Leisch
Treasurer:
Kurt Hornik
Member at Large:
John Chambers
In addition, Peter Dalgaard and Martin Mächler were elected as auditors.
